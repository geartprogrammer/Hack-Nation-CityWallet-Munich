How it works

1
Add to your wallet — the pass installs in Apple Wallet or Google Wallet. No app download.
2
Walk near the cafe — your phone detects you're nearby and shows the pass on your lock screen. Automatically.
3
Show the barcode — the barista scans it. Done. The pass updates to "Redeemed."
